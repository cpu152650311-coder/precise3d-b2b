Distribution

EMEA Distributor: 0 to 2,000 Units in 18 Months

Germany | Pro X1 & Creator C1

Challenge

A Frankfurt-based electronics distributor wanted to add 3D printers to their catalog but had no in-house 3D printing expertise. They needed a turnkey product line with local-language documentation and CE-certified hardware.

Solution

Pro X1 and Creator C1 units with German firmware UI, DE packaging, and DE quick-start guides. 3-day on-site training for 8 sales engineers. Co-branded marketing kit (banners, datasheets, demo videos).

Results

2,000+ units in 18 months | 5 retail chains onboarded | <1% return rate

North American Brand: Custom Firmware & Rebrand in 8 Weeks

USA | Creator C1 OEM Variant

Challenge

A US consumer electronics brand wanted to launch a house-brand 3D printer to compete with Bambu Lab and Creality at retail. They needed custom firmware UI, branded enclosure, and retail-ready packaging within 3 months.

Solution

Custom boot screen and UI theme, silk-screened enclosure with brand logo, FCC + UL pre-certification. Retail packaging with brand colors, English-only quick-start guide, and US power cord. 3,000-unit pilot batch.

Results

Pilot batch sold out in 6 weeks | 10,000-unit follow-on order | 4.2/5 Amazon rating

Education

Southeast Asia STEM Program: 500 Schools in 12 Countries

Philippines | Start S1 Education Bundle

Challenge

A regional education NGO needed affordable, safe, and easy-to-use 3D printers for a multi-country STEM rollout. Budget constraints, tropical climate (humidity), and varying power quality required robust engineering.

Solution

Start S1 with fully enclosed chamber, filament run-out sensor, and auto-resume after power loss. Teacher training curriculum in 5 languages. 2-year warranty with local spare parts depots.

Results

500+ schools equipped | 20,000+ students engaged | 98.5% uptime in first year

Brazil | Full Product Line
