The 6-Step Process

Every Stage Defined. Every Deliverable Clear.

Each step has a defined timeline, clear responsibilities, and concrete deliverables. You will always know what happens next and when.

1

Submit Your Inquiry

Time: 5 minutes · Response: within 24 hours

Fill out the inquiry form with your basic information: company name, target market, estimated volume, and program interest (distribution, OEM, education). No commitment — this is purely a conversation starter. Our partnership team reviews every inquiry within 24 hours and responds with initial information tailored to your situation.

Your Responsibility

  • Complete the inquiry form
  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Share your market and goals

Our Deliverable

  • Response within 24 hours
  • Product catalog and spec sheets
  • Preliminary pricing indication
2

Requirements & Proposal

Time: 1-3 business days

After initial contact, we schedule a detailed discussion (call or email, your preference). We cover: target market analysis, product model selection, volume projections, certification requirements, packaging needs, branding preferences (if OEM), and shipping destination. Based on this discussion, we prepare a comprehensive proposal with pricing, MOQ, lead times, and terms.

Your Responsibility

  • Participate in discovery call
  • Share certification requirements
  • Clarify volume and timeline needs

Our Deliverable

  • Written commercial proposal
  • Detailed pricing by volume tier
  • Shipping estimate for your region
  • Sample order terms (if applicable)
3

Sample Order & Evaluation

Time: 2-3 weeks (incl. shipping)

Order 1-5 sample units at wholesale pricing with zero long-term commitment. Test the printers yourself — evaluate print quality, reliability, user experience, and packaging. Show them to potential customers. Our team provides full technical support during your evaluation. Most partners confirm their product-market fit within 2 weeks of receiving samples. If the product is not right for your market, there is no obligation to proceed.

Your Responsibility

  • Place sample order (1-5 units)
  • Evaluate products thoroughly
  • Gather customer/market feedback
  • Confirm go/no-go decision

Our Deliverable

  • Sample units at wholesale pricing
  • Technical support during evaluation
  • Spec sheets and certifications
  • Marketing material access

Three product tiers, one standard of quality. Request samples of any or all models.

4

Order Confirmation & Production

Time: 2-3 weeks production

Once you confirm your first commercial order, we issue a proforma invoice with final pricing, payment terms (typically T/T 30/70), and confirmed delivery date. Production begins immediately upon deposit receipt. During production, you receive weekly progress updates. Your dedicated account manager is your single point of contact throughout. For OEM orders, packaging design and branding proofs are approved before production starts.

Your Responsibility

  • Confirm order and sign agreement
  • Submit deposit payment (30%)
  • Approve packaging/branding proofs
  • Confirm shipping method and destination

Our Deliverable

  • Proforma invoice and timeline
  • Weekly production status updates
  • Branding proofs (OEM orders)
  • Confirmed shipping date
5

Quality Control & Pre-Shipment

Time: 3-5 days (parallel with production)

Every unit in your order passes through our complete 7-gate QC process before shipping. 100% print-tested. Test print log included in the box. OQC sampling audit conducted per AQL 0.65 tightened inspection. We can provide photos and test reports for your order before shipment — just ask. Final balance payment (70%) is due after QC clearance and before shipment release.

Your Responsibility

  • Review QC reports (optional)
  • Submit balance payment (70%)
  • Confirm shipping arrangements
  • Provide any final documentation needs

Our Deliverable

  • QC test reports and photos (on request)
  • Certificate of conformance
  • Packing list and shipping docs
  • Full shipment photo documentation
6

Shipping & Delivery

Time: 3-40 days (method-dependent)

Your order ships via your chosen method. Tracking number provided within 24 hours of departure. All documents (commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading/AWB, certificate of origin, certifications) included. Upon delivery, verify the shipment against the packing list and inspect for any transit damage. We remain available for after-sales support, technical questions, spare parts ordering, and reordering.

Your Responsibility

  • Handle import customs (or use DAP)
  • Verify shipment upon arrival
  • Report any transit damage promptly
  • Begin selling and reordering

Our Deliverable

  • Tracking number and shipping docs
  • Automated milestone notifications
  • After-sales technical support
  • Spare parts availability
  • Reordering and restocking support

Common Questions

Partner FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from new partners.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our standard MOQ is 50 units for distribution partners. Sample orders of 1-5 units are available at wholesale pricing for evaluation purposes with no long-term commitment. OEM/white label programs may have different MOQ requirements depending on the scope of customization. Contact our team for a detailed discussion of your specific situation.

How long from inquiry to delivery?

+

The complete timeline is typically 4-8 weeks: 1-2 days for initial response and proposal, 2-3 weeks for sample evaluation (including shipping), 2-3 weeks for production after order confirmation, and 3-40 days for shipping depending on the method. Air express can deliver in 3-7 days; sea freight takes 25-40 days. See our detailed timeline breakdown above.

Can I customize printers with my brand?

+

Yes, through our OEM and white label program. Customization options include: logo application on the printer body and enclosure, custom firmware boot screen and UI theming, branded packaging design, custom color options, and customized accessory kits. MOQ and one-time tooling/setup fees apply depending on customization scope. Most branding projects require 4-6 weeks for initial setup.

What certifications do the printers carry?

+

All Precise3D printers carry CE (EU EMC + LVD + RoHS), FCC Part 15 (US), UKCA (UK), and RoHS 2.0 certifications. Our factory holds ISO 9001:2015 certification from TUV Rheinland. Additional certifications including UL/ETL (US/Canada safety), RCM (Australia), and BIS (India) are available on request. Certification documentation package is provided with every shipment.

What about after-sales support?

+

We provide comprehensive after-sales support including: 12-month warranty on all printers, detailed technical documentation and service manuals, partner training programs (self-paced video + live sessions), dedicated account manager for business matters, technical support email with 24-hour response time, spare parts with 3-month forward stock, and consumables always in stock (nozzles, build plates, belts, hotends). We also provide quarterly business reviews for distribution partners.

What payment terms do you offer?

+

Standard payment terms for new partners: T/T 30% deposit with order confirmation, 70% balance before shipment. After establishing a trading history (typically 3+ orders), we can discuss more flexible terms including L/C at sight, and for long-term high-volume partners, net-30 terms may be available. All payments in USD. Sample orders can be paid 100% in advance via T/T or wire transfer.
