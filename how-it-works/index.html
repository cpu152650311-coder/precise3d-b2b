Partner Journey

How It Works

From your first inquiry to your first shipment — and beyond. Here's what the Precise3D partner journey looks like.

1

Submit Your Inquiry

Tell us about your market, channels, and goals. No commitment — just a conversation starter. You'll hear back within 24 hours.

Time: 5 minutes

2

Discovery Call

A 30-minute call with our partnership team. We'll discuss your market, our program tiers, and whether there's a mutual fit. No hard sell — we're evaluating the fit as much as you are.

Time: 30 minutes (within 2–3 days of inquiry)

3

Sample Order & Evaluation

Order 1–5 sample units at wholesale pricing. Test them yourself. Show them to potential customers. Zero commitment beyond the sample order. Most partners know within 2 weeks whether the product fits their market.

Time: 2–3 weeks (including shipping)

4

Partnership Agreement

Review and sign the distributor agreement. Standard terms, no hidden clauses. Territory boundaries, margin structure, MAP policy, and mutual commitments are clearly defined. Typical agreement: 2 pages.

Time: 3–5 business days

5

Team Training

Your sales and support team completes Precise3D's training program. Self-paced videos + live virtual sessions. Your team becomes product-certified in 2–3 weeks. Marketing assets and partner portal access granted.

Time: 2–3 weeks

6

First Commercial Order

Place your first inventory order. Production: 2–3 weeks. Shipping: 3–30 days depending on method. Your units arrive pre-calibrated, certified, and retail-ready.

Time: 3–6 weeks

7

Launch & Ongoing Growth

Start selling. We support you with marketing, technical questions, and quarterly business reviews. Your dedicated account manager monitors your growth and helps you scale.

Time: Ongoing
