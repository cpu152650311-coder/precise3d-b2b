Pricing & MOQ

What to Expect

Transparent information about how our pricing works, what MOQ means for your business, and what factors influence final pricing.

Volume-Tiered Pricing

All Precise3D printers use volume-tiered wholesale pricing. Higher monthly or annual commitment volumes unlock progressively better unit pricing. We do not publish a fixed price list because pricing varies by market, program type, and volume. Typical tier breaks: 50-99 units/month, 100-299 units/month, 300+ units/month. Annual framework agreements provide the best rates.

Minimum Order Quantity

Standard MOQ: 50 units for first distribution order, with mixed-model orders accepted. Sample evaluation: 1-5 units at wholesale pricing with no long-term commitment. OEM white-label: MOQ varies by customization scope (typically 100-500 units depending on tooling). Education bundles: flexible MOQ with classroom sets of 6-12 units.

What Affects Pricing

Key factors: order volume (larger = lower unit cost), program type (OEM has setup costs but lower ongoing pricing), customization scope (branding, packaging, firmware), certifications required (additional testing fees may apply), shipping method (FOB vs. CIF vs. DAP), and payment terms (T/T, L/C). We provide full transparency on all cost components in your quote.

Sample Order Pricing

Sample units (1-5 pcs) are priced at wholesale rates — the same rate as a 50-unit order, not retail. This allows you to evaluate the product at your actual cost of goods. Shipping is charged at cost for samples (air express recommended, 3-7 days to most destinations). Sample cost is credited toward your first commercial order if you proceed within 90 days.

Common Questions

Quote & Pricing FAQ

What is the MOQ for a first order?

+

Standard MOQ is 50 units for distribution partners. Sample evaluation orders of 1-5 units are available at wholesale pricing with no long-term commitment. OEM white-label programs may require higher MOQ (typically 100-500 units) depending on the scope of customization required. We are flexible — tell us about your situation and we will find a workable starting point.

How quickly will I receive my quote?

+

All inquiries receive an acknowledgment within 2 hours and an initial response within 24 hours. A detailed written quote with pricing by volume tier, lead times, and shipping estimates is typically delivered within 1-2 business days after we have a clear understanding of your requirements. Complex OEM projects with extensive customization may take 3-5 business days for a full quote.

What do I need to provide for an accurate quote?

+

For the most accurate quote, tell us: your target market/country, estimated monthly or initial order volume, preferred product models, program type (distribution/OEM/education), and any special requirements such as branding, certifications, or packaging preferences. Providing this information upfront helps us deliver a precise quote without multiple rounds of back-and-forth.

Are there discounts for larger orders?

+

Yes. Our pricing is volume-tiered with progressively better unit pricing at higher volumes. Annual framework agreements with committed volumes provide the best rates along with additional benefits: priority production scheduling, dedicated account management, co-marketing support, and extended payment terms after trading history is established. We invest in partners who commit to growth.

Do quoted prices include shipping?

+

Standard quotes are FOB Shenzhen (you arrange and pay for freight). We provide separate freight estimates for air, sea, and express shipping to your destination port or door so you can compare costs and choose the most cost-effective option. CIF (we arrange freight and insurance to your port) and DAP (door-to-door delivery) terms are also available on request.
