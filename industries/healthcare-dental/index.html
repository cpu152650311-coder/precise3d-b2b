Precision for
Patient Care

Medical-grade accuracy for dental models, surgical guides, and anatomical replicas. Validated workflows, biocompatible materials, and ISO 13485-aware manufacturing.

Applications

Dental & Medical Use Cases

Dental Models

High-resolution study models, crown and bridge models, and orthodontic diagnostic casts. 50-micron layer resolution captures fine anatomical detail. Compatible with intraoral scan workflows (STL export).

Surgical Guides

Patient-specific surgical guides from CT/CBCT data. Autoclavable materials available. Dimensional accuracy verified against digital plan within ±0.1mm.

Anatomical Replicas

Full-color or single-color anatomical models for surgical planning and patient education. Multi-material printing for bone, tissue, and vasculature differentiation.

Technical Requirements

What Healthcare Needs

Material Requirements

Biocompatible filaments (ISO 10993 tested). Sterilizable materials (autoclave at 134°C). Low-odor printing for clinical environments. Food-safe and skin-safe certifications where required.

Accuracy Standards

Dimensional accuracy within ±0.1mm at 50-micron layer height. Z-axis accuracy critical for occlusal surfaces. Enclosed chamber for thermal stability during multi-hour prints.

Workflow Integration

Seamless STL import from intraoral scanners (3Shape, iTero, Medit). Pre-configured slicer profiles for dental applications. Batch printing support for multi-case overnight runs.

Regulatory Awareness

ISO 13485-aware manufacturing. Full material traceability per batch. Device master record documentation available. CE marking for medical devices where applicable.
