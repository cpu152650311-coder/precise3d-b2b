AEC Use Cases

Technical Fit

What AEC Professionals Need

Large Build Volume

Pro X1: 256×256×256mm for detailed components. Multi-part assembly for larger models — alignment pins, snap-fit connections, and adhesive bonding guides. Split models for printing bigger than build volume.

Dimensional Accuracy

±0.1mm accuracy for scale model consistency. XY and Z calibration documented. Consistent across multi-part assemblies — parts designed in CAD fit together as designed when printed separately.

Multi-Day Reliability

Power-loss recovery for uninterrupted 24-72 hour prints. Filament run-out detection with auto-pause. Thermal runaway protection. Enclosed chamber for stable ambient conditions during long jobs.

CAD Workflow

Direct STL export from Revit, SketchUp, Rhino, and ArchiCAD. Pre-configured slicer profiles for architectural models — optimized for flat surfaces and sharp edges. Batch export and print management.
