Applications

Jewelry & Fashion Use Cases

Lost-Wax Casting

Print high-detail wax-like patterns for investment casting in gold, silver, and platinum. 0.2mm nozzle option captures filigree, stone settings, and fine text. Clean burnout with minimal ash residue.

Design Prototypes

Rapidly iterate ring, pendant, and bracelet designs before committing to precious metals. Share physical prototypes with clients for approval. Reduce design cycle from weeks to hours.

Fashion Accessories

Custom buttons, buckles, embellishments, and wearable tech housings. Multi-material options for flexible (TPU) and rigid (PLA/PETG) components. Prototype to small-batch production.

Technical Specs

What Jewelers Need

Resolution & Detail

Minimum 0.05mm layer height with 0.2mm nozzle. Captures stone-setting details, texture patterns, and engraved text. Smooth top surfaces reduce post-processing time before casting.

Castable Materials

Validated castable filaments compatible with standard investment casting. Low-ash burnout (residue <0.1%). Tested with multiple investment powders and casting houses. Documentation included.

Multi-Material

Dual extruder option for soluble supports — print complex geometries (interlocking rings, hollow pendants) without manual support removal. Water-dissolvable PVA supports for delicate designs.

Surface Finish

Ironing mode for smooth top surfaces. Adaptive layer height for curved surfaces — thinner layers on slopes, thicker on verticals. Post-processing guide for vapor smoothing and polishing.
