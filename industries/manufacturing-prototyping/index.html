Use Cases

How Manufacturers Use Our Printers

Rapid Prototyping

Iterate designs in hours instead of waiting weeks for outsourced prototypes. Test fit, form, and function before committing to tooling. Pro X1 delivers ±0.1mm accuracy for engineering validation.

Jigs & Fixtures

Custom assembly jigs, drilling guides, and inspection fixtures printed overnight. Replace expensive CNC-machined fixtures at 1/10th the cost. Engineering materials (ABS, Nylon, PC) for shop-floor durability.

End-Use Parts

Low-volume production parts without tooling investment. Suitable for brackets, housings, covers, and custom connectors. Nylon and PC materials for functional requirements — heat, impact, and chemical resistance.

Technical Fit

Industrial Requirements Met

Material Portfolio

ABS (impact resistance), ASA (UV-stable outdoor parts), Nylon (wear-resistant gears/bearings), Polycarbonate (high-temp to 110°C), PETG-CF (stiffness + lightweight), TPU (flexible seals/gaskets). All with validated print profiles.

Accuracy & Repeatability

CoreXY kinematics with linear rails deliver consistent ±0.1mm accuracy across the full build volume. Enclosed chamber ensures thermal stability. Automatic calibration routines maintain accuracy between prints.

Workflow Integration

STEP file import for direct CAD-to-print. Network printing via Wi-Fi or Ethernet. Job queue management for batch production. Remote monitoring with camera and failure detection.

Production Reliability

Designed for 24/7 operation. Power-loss recovery — resumes exactly where it stopped. Filament run-out detection pauses and waits for reload. 4-hour continuous test print on every unit before shipping.
