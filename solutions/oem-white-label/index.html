Why White Label

Build Your Brand on a Battle-Tested Platform

Developing a 3D printer from scratch costs $500K–$2M in R&D, takes 12–18 months, and carries significant technical risk. The alternative — reselling generic ODMs with a logo sticker — produces an undifferentiated product that competes on price alone.

Precise3D's OEM program offers a third path: a genuinely engineered platform that you customize. Start with a proven CoreXY architecture, all-metal hotend, and pre-tuned firmware. Add your enclosure design, your colorways, your packaging, your brand identity. Ship in 6–8 weeks from PO.

Four-stage customization: base platform → branding → packaging → market
