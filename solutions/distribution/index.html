The Precise3D Difference

Territory Protection Is Real. Here's How It Works.

Many manufacturers promise territory protection. Few deliver. Here is Precise3D's binding commitment to Authorized Distributors and Master Distributors:

1 
No direct sales in your territory. We will not sell to end customers in countries where you hold distribution rights. Inquiries routed to your sales team.
2 
MAP enforcement. Minimum Advertised Price policy applies to all channels. We monitor and enforce. Your margins are protected from price erosion.
3 
Amazon marketplace management. We control brand registry on major marketplaces. Unauthorized sellers are removed. Your listing is the authorized listing in your territory.
4 
Serial number tracking. Every unit is traceable to its authorized distributor. Gray market units are identified and their source is held accountable.
