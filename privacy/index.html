Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, company name, phone number, and messages when you submit our inquiry form. We also collect standard web server log data: IP address, browser type, referring page, and time of visit. This is used for site analytics and security purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries about distribution partnerships, OEM services, and product information. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your inquiry data is stored securely and only accessed by our partnership team.

3. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies. We use only essential session cookies required for form submission functionality. No consent banner is needed as no non-essential cookies are deployed.

4. Data Storage & Security

Form submissions are transmitted securely (HTTPS) and stored on Cloudflare Workers infrastructure. We retain inquiry data for 24 months to maintain business relationship continuity. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare processes visitor data as part of content delivery and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to their processing. Our inquiry form submissions are processed through a Cloudflare Worker. No data is sent to third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing platforms.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and object to processing of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.
