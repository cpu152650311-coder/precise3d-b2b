Shipping & Logistics

Export-grade packaging engineered for ocean and air freight. Door-to-door or port-to-port. Full customs documentation support for every major market.

Packaging

Built to Survive the Journey

Custom Foam Inserts

High-density EPE foam, CNC-cut to each printer model. Absorbs shock and vibration. Every insert tested to ISTA 3A simulation standards: drop, vibration, and compression.

Double-Wall Cartons

Export-grade 5-ply corrugated cartons with reinforced corners. Moisture-resistant coating. Desiccant packs and humidity indicator cards included for sea freight shipments.

Palletized & Secured

Standard configuration: 12 units per pallet, stretch-wrapped with edge protectors. FCL 20ft: up to 120 units. FCL 40ft: up to 200 units. Tilt and shock indicators on every pallet.
