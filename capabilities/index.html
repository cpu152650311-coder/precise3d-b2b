R&D Infrastructure

Where Your Product Is Engineered

Mechanical Design Lab

SolidWorks and Fusion 360 for parametric design. FEA simulation for structural rigidity and thermal analysis. In-house CNC machining for rapid prototypes. Full kinematics testing rigs for CoreXY, bedslinger, and delta architectures.

Quality & Certifications

Global Standards, Factory-Direct Quality

Every Precise3D product is manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 certified processes and certified to the international standards your market requires. We manage the full certification lifecycle — including variant re-certification for customized models.

Quality Control System

Seven-stage QC process from incoming component inspection to final packaging audit. Each printer undergoes a 4-hour burn-in test printing a standardized benchmark model. Critical components (motion system, heated bed, power supply) receive 100% inspection. Statistical process control (SPC) tracks defect rates across all production lines.

  • ✓Incoming component inspection (IQC)
  • ✓In-process QC at 5 production checkpoints
  • ✓4-hour burn-in test with benchmark print
  • ✓Final QA audit with dimensional accuracy verification
  • ✓Pre-shipment inspection (PSI) per AQL 2.5

Equipment & Metrology

Our QC lab is equipped with calibrated metrology instruments for dimensional verification, electrical safety testing, and environmental stress screening. All equipment is maintained under ISO 17025-calibrated standards with documented traceability.

  • ✓CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) for dimensional accuracy
  • ✓Hi-pot tester for electrical safety (dielectric withstand)
  • ✓Thermal camera for heated bed uniformity verification
  • ✓Sound level meter for acoustic compliance testing
  • ✓Vibration analyzer for motion system characterization

How We Work With You

The OEM Partnership Process

From initial specification to mass production, our structured process keeps your project on time and on budget. Typical timeline: 12-20 weeks from spec sign-off to first shipment.

1. Specification

You define your requirements: build volume, speed targets, materials support, enclosure design, branding, firmware, target certifications, and market price point. We translate this into a technical specification document with bill of materials (BOM), timeline, and MOQ estimate within 5 business days.

2. Engineering Sample

We build 3-5 prototype units incorporating all agreed customizations. These samples are fully functional and represent the production-intent design. You review, test, and provide feedback. Typically 4-6 weeks from specification approval.

3. Certification & Pilot

We manage certification testing for your target markets (CE, FCC, KC, etc.) with accredited third-party labs. A 50-100 unit pilot production run validates the full manufacturing process. You give final approval before mass production ramp-up.
