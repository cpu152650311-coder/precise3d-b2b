Print Profiles

Pre-Tuned for Every Material

Temperature Towers

Optimal nozzle and bed temperature determined through systematic testing. Temperature tower printed for every filament, every batch. Documented sweet spot for layer adhesion vs. stringing.

Retraction Tuning

Retraction distance and speed calibrated per material. Direct drive and Bowden profiles available. Tested across 10+ retraction test models to minimize stringing.

Speed & Acceleration

Maximum volumetric flow rate measured for each material. Speed and acceleration limits set to ensure consistent extrusion. Separate profiles for quality (60mm/s) and speed (150mm/s+).

Cooling Optimization

Part cooling fan speed calibrated per material and layer. Overhang performance optimized. Bridging fan settings tuned for clean spans up to 50mm.

Bed Adhesion

First layer settings optimized per material and build surface. Brim, skirt, and raft recommendations. Bed surface recommendation: PEI, glass, or adhesive.

Drying Protocols

Drying requirements documented per material: temperature, duration, and storage conditions. Filament dryness indicators and recommended dryer models.
