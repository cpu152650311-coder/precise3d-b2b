Customization Scope

What You Can Customize

Every subsystem of the printer is configurable. Choose the components and features that differentiate your brand in your target market.

Enclosure & Frame

Full enclosure customization from concept to production. Options include color-matched sheet metal panels (RAL color system or Pantone matching), laser-engraved or silk-screened logos on front/side/top panels, custom material selection (powder-coated steel, anodized aluminum, acrylic windows, tinted polycarbonate), branded front bezel and touchscreen frame, and ambient LED lighting with your brand color. All enclosure tooling owned by you after project completion.

Extruder & Hotend

Choice of direct drive or Bowden extrusion system. All-metal hotend (up to 300-degree-C for engineering materials) or PTFE-lined (up to 260-degree-C for standard materials). Single or dual extruder configuration. High-flow hotend options (CHT nozzle, volcano-style) for speed-focused models. Custom toolhead PCB with integrated part cooling, nozzle camera mount, and accelerometer for input shaping. Hardened steel nozzle for abrasive filaments.

Electronics & Controller

32-bit ARM (STM32F103/F407) or 64-bit controller board selection. TMC stepper driver choice: TMC2209 (silent, UART), TMC2226 (silent, higher current), or TMC5160 (high-power, SPI with sensorless homing). Connectivity options: Wi-Fi 2.4/5GHz, Ethernet RJ45, USB-C data. Regional power supply (110-120V or 220-240V, 50/60Hz) with appropriate plug type. Touchscreen display: 3.5-inch, 4.3-inch, 5-inch, or 7-inch options. Custom controller board with your branding and pinout.

Build Platform

Build volume customization within the mechanical limits of the frame architecture. Build surface options: smooth PEI spring steel (single or double-sided), textured PEI, carborundum glass, or polycarbonate flex plate. Heated bed power configurable (200W-750W) for different max temperatures (100-120-degree-C). Bed leveling: inductive probe, BLTouch/CR Touch, strain gauge, or manual. Magnetic or clip-based build plate attachment.

Branding & Identity

Every customer-facing surface carries your brand, not ours. Branding package includes: front panel logo (laser-engraved, silk-screened, or metal badge), side panel brand name/graphic, branded boot screen and UI theme, custom start-up sound, branded packaging with full-color print, branded quick-start guide and user manual (multi-language), branded SD card/USB drive label, and branded accessory box. Zero Precise3D reference on any customer-facing material.

Packaging & Unboxing

Professional packaging design as a brand touchpoint. Full-color printed outer box with your branding and product photography. Custom foam insert layout engineered for drop protection (ISTA 3A transit testing). Multi-language quick-start guide (printed, 12+ languages available). Accessory kit presentation with branded boxes. Sustainable packaging options: recycled cardboard, soy-based inks, reduced plastic. Retail-ready or bulk/e-commerce packaging configurations.

Color & Finish

Color Matching & Surface Finishes

Your brand has a specific color palette. We match it precisely across all printer surfaces using industrial color systems and provide physical finish samples for approval before production.

Color Systems

We match to RAL Classic, RAL Design, Pantone C/U, and custom formulations. Powder coating for metal panels: matte, satin, semi-gloss, and textured finishes available. Anodizing for aluminum parts in natural, black, and custom colors. Injection-molded plastic parts in any color with color chip approval. Color consistency verified with spectrophotometer across production batches within Delta E less than or equal to 1.5.

Finish Options

Powder coat: smooth matte, fine texture, coarse texture, hammer tone, metallic. Anodized: clear, brushed + clear, sandblasted + colored, hard coat. Plastic: matte, gloss, soft-touch, metallic flake. Decals and overlays: die-cut vinyl, screen-printed polycarbonate, domed resin badges, metal nameplates with etched/painted lettering. Physical finish sample cards provided for approval.

Branding Techniques

Laser engraving: precise, permanent marking on metal and coated surfaces. Silk screening: multi-color printing on panels with Pantone-matched inks. UV digital printing: full-color, photo-quality graphics on flat surfaces. Pad printing: for small logos on irregular surfaces. Embossed/debossed logos in molded plastic parts. Metal badge attachment with adhesive or mechanical fasteners. All techniques tested for durability (abrasion, cleaning chemicals, UV exposure).

OEM Process

From Brief to Production: The 5-Phase Process

Our structured OEM process keeps your project on schedule and on budget. A dedicated project manager guides you through each phase with clear deliverables, timelines, and approval gates.

Phase 1: Specification

1-2 weeks

You provide: brand guidelines, target market, feature priorities, target retail price point, and target certifications. We translate this into a Technical Specification Document (TSD) with: complete bill of materials (BOM) listing every component and its supplier, preliminary 3D renders of the customized enclosure, firmware customization scope, estimated unit cost at target volumes, and project timeline with milestones.

Phase 2: Engineering Sample

4-6 weeks

We build 3-5 fully functional prototype units incorporating all agreed customizations. These are production-intent samples — not rough prototypes. Each sample ships to you for evaluation. Deliverables include: physical samples with all customizations, print quality test results (Benchy, dimensional accuracy, overhang, bridging), firmware with your branded boot screen and UI, packaging mockup, and sample review checklist.

Phase 3: Certification

3-8 weeks

We manage the full certification process with accredited third-party labs (TUV, SGS, Intertek, UL). Certifications covered: CE (EMC + LVD + RED for Wi-Fi models), FCC (Part 15B for USA), UKCA, KC (Korea), RCM (Australia/NZ), and additional market-specific certifications. We handle lab submission, test monitoring, failure remediation, and documentation. Certification certificates issued in your company name.

Phase 4: Pilot Production

2-3 weeks

50-100 unit pilot production run on the actual production line with production tooling, production workers, and full QC process. This is the final validation before mass production. Deliverables: pilot run QC report (yield rate, defect categories, corrective actions), production BOM locked with supplier lead times, finalized packaging with production-quality printing, final firmware build signed and checksum-verified.

Phase 5: Mass Production

4-8 weeks per PO

Full production runs per your purchase order volume. Capacity: 5,000+ units/month across three assembly lines. Standard QC includes 100% functional testing (4-hour burn-in print), AQL 2.5 sampling for cosmetic inspection, and pre-shipment inspection with detailed report. Ongoing engineering support for component EOL management, continuous improvement, and next-generation development.

Quality Assurance

Quality Control for Custom Builds

Customized products require customized quality control. Our QC process adapts to your specific configuration, ensuring that every custom component — from the colored enclosure panel to the branded firmware build — meets specification.

Custom QC Checklist

Every OEM variant has its own QC checklist, derived from the technical specification document. This includes all standard checks (motion system, heated bed, extrusion, safety) plus customization-specific checks: color match verification (Delta E less than or equal to 1.5 vs. approved sample), logo position measurement (within 0.5mm of specification), firmware build verification (checksum match, boot screen content, UI theme), packaging content verification (all branded items present per BOM), and certification label placement and legibility.

Sample Approval Process

Before each production run, we ship you a golden sample — a production-line unit that represents the exact specification for the entire run. You review and sign off on: enclosure color and finish, logo placement and quality, firmware branding (boot screen, UI, slicer profiles), packaging quality, print quality from the production-line burn-in test, and all accessories and documentation. Production does not begin until you approve the golden sample in writing.

Certification Management

Global Certifications Under Your Brand

We manage the full certification lifecycle for your customized product. You receive certificates in your company name, valid for your specific product variant.

Certification Portfolio

Standard certification packages: CE Marking (EU), FCC (USA), UKCA (UK), KC (Korea), RCM (Australia/NZ). Additional certifications available: IC (Canada), MIC (Japan), NCC (Taiwan), BIS (India), EAC (Eurasian Union), SABS (South Africa). RoHS, REACH, and WEEE compliance documentation. UL/ETL safety listing available for North American retail partners.

Variant Certification

When a certified base platform is customized, many certifications require re-testing or a variant assessment. We manage this process: determine which changes trigger re-certification (enclosure color changes typically do not; power supply or Wi-Fi module changes do), submit variant application to the certification body, manage any required delta testing, and deliver updated certificates under your brand. Typical variant certification timeline: 3-8 weeks.

Documentation Package

Complete regulatory documentation package delivered to you: certificate PDFs from accredited labs, test reports (EMC, LVD, RED, safety), Declaration of Conformity (DoC) template with your company details, technical construction file (TCF) for each certified variant, labeling artwork files (CE mark, FCC ID, UKCA mark, etc.), and user manual safety sections in required languages. All documents maintained and updated for the product lifecycle.
