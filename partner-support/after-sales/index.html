Warranty & RMA

Standard Warranty

12 months on printer, 3 months on consumable parts (nozzles, build plates). Covers manufacturing defects. Does not cover user damage or consumable wear.

RMA Process

Partner submits RMA request via portal → Precise3D reviews within 24h → approved RMAs ship replacement within 48h. Advance replacement available for critical failures (printer won't power on, failed mainboard).

DOA Handling

Dead-on-arrival units are replaced immediately with no diagnostic requirement. We trust your assessment. Return label provided; replacement ships same day.

Spare Parts Program

Recommended Inventory

For every 100 units sold, we recommend stocking: 5 hotend assemblies, 10 nozzle kits, 5 build plate sheets, 3 mainboards, 3 PSUs, 10 belt kits. Total stocking cost: ~$800. Covers 95% of typical warranty and repair scenarios.

Parts Ordering

All spare parts available at wholesale cost. No minimum order. Ships within 48 hours via DHL Express. Emergency parts can ship same-day for critical customer situations.

Parts Catalog

Exploded-view diagrams with part numbers for every assembly. Available in PDF and interactive web format. Updated with each hardware revision so you're never ordering the wrong part.
