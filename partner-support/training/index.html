Training Program

Four Learning Tracks. Self-Paced or Live.

Track 1: 3D Printing Fundamentals

Duration: 4 hours • Format: Self-paced video + quiz

FDM vs. Resin vs. SLS. CoreXY vs. Cartesian vs. Delta. Material families and their applications. The vocabulary and concepts your team needs to have credible conversations with technical buyers.

Track 2: Precise3D Product Certification

Duration: 6 hours • Format: Live virtual + hands-on

Deep-dive on Pro X1 and Creator C1: features, specs, setup, calibration, material profiles, firmware. Includes hands-on exercises with sample units. Certification exam at the end.

Track 3: Competitive Sales Playbook

Duration: 3 hours • Format: Live virtual + battle cards

Head-to-head comparison against Bambu Lab, Creality, QIDI, Anycubic, Prusa. Feature-by-feature advantages. Objection handling scripts. Pricing and value justification frameworks.

Track 4: Technical Troubleshooting

Duration: 5 hours • Format: Live virtual + knowledge base

Common print quality issues and root causes. Mechanical troubleshooting (belts, bearings, hotend). Firmware diagnostics. When to repair vs. replace vs. escalate to Precise3D engineering.

Certification

Precise3D Certified Partner Badge

Teams that complete all four training tracks earn the Precise3D Certified Partner designation. The certification badge is authorized for use on your website, sales materials, and email signatures. It signals to technical buyers that your team can answer their questions — not just take their order.

Certification must be renewed annually with a 2-hour update session covering new products, firmware features, and updated competitive intelligence. 85% of certified partners report increased B2B sales conversion within 6 months of certification.
