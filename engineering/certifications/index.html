Certification Register

Complete Certification Portfolio

FCC

FCC Part 15B (USA)

Federal Communications Commission — electromagnetic interference compliance for the United States. Covers radiated and conducted emissions. Required for all electronic devices sold in the US market.

Active
CE

CE Mark (EU / EEA)

Conformité Européenne — covers EMC Directive 2014/30/EU, Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU, and RoHS Directive 2011/65/EU. Required for sale in all 27 EU member states plus EEA countries.

Active
UKCA

UKCA Mark (United Kingdom)

UK Conformity Assessed — post-Brexit replacement for CE marking in Great Britain. Covers equivalent EMC, electrical safety, and RoHS requirements under UK statutory instruments. Maintained in parallel with CE.

Active
UL

UL 62368-1 (Audio/Video & IT Equipment Safety)

Underwriters Laboratories safety standard for IT and communication equipment. Covers electrical shock, fire, mechanical, and thermal hazards. Recognized in US and Canada (cUL). Required by most US retailers and institutional buyers.

Active
RoHS

RoHS 2.0 (EU 2011/65/EU + Amendment 2015/863)

Restriction of Hazardous Substances — limits lead, mercury, cadmium, hexavalent chromium, PBBs, PBDEs, and four phthalates (DEHP, BBP, DBP, DIBP). Full compliance verified by third-party lab testing on every production batch.

Active
REACH

REACH (EC 1907/2006)

Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals — EU regulation covering chemical substances in products. All materials and components used in Precise3D printers are REACH-compliant with SVHC (Substances of Very High Concern) declarations available.

Active
EN 62368

EN 62368-1 (EU Safety Standard)

European harmonized safety standard for audio/video and IT equipment — the EU counterpart to UL 62368-1. Covers hazard-based safety engineering for electrical, mechanical, thermal, and radiation risks. Required for CE marking.
