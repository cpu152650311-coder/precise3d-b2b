Certification Register

Complete Certification Portfolio

FCC

FCC Part 15B (USA)

Federal Communications Commission — electromagnetic interference compliance for the United States. Covers radiated and conducted emissions. Required for all electronic devices sold in the US market. Testing performed at TUV Rheinland accredited lab.

Active
CE

CE Mark (EU / EEA)

Conformite Europeenne — covers EMC Directive 2014/30/EU, Low Voltage Directive 2014/35/EU, and RoHS Directive 2011/65/EU. Required for sale in all 27 EU member states plus EEA countries. Declaration of Conformity maintained and available on request.

Active
UKCA

UKCA Mark (United Kingdom)

UK Conformity Assessed — post-Brexit replacement for CE marking in Great Britain. Covers equivalent EMC, electrical safety, and RoHS requirements under UK statutory instruments. Maintained in parallel with CE for dual-market access.

Active
UL

UL 62368-1 (Audio/Video and IT Equipment Safety)

Underwriters Laboratories safety standard for IT and communication equipment. Covers electrical shock, fire, mechanical, and thermal hazards. Recognized in US and Canada (cUL). Required by most US retailers and institutional buyers for product liability insurance.

Active
RoHS

RoHS 2.0 (EU 2011/65/EU plus Amendment 2015/863)

Restriction of Hazardous Substances — limits lead, mercury, cadmium, hexavalent chromium, PBBs, PBDEs, and four phthalates (DEHP, BBP, DBP, DIBP). Full compliance verified by third-party lab testing (SGS) on every production batch.

Active
REACH

REACH (EC 1907/2006)

Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals — EU regulation covering chemical substances in products. All materials and components used in Precise3D printers are REACH-compliant with SVHC (Substances of Very High Concern) declarations available for every BOM item.

Active
EN 62368

EN 62368-1 (EU Safety Standard)

European harmonized safety standard for audio/video and IT equipment — the EU counterpart to UL 62368-1. Covers hazard-based safety engineering for electrical, mechanical, thermal, and radiation risks. Mandatory for CE marking under the LVD.

Quality Management

ISO 9001:2015 — The System Behind the Certificates

Product certifications prove compliance at a point in time. ISO 9001 proves that compliance is maintained over time — through documented processes, internal audits, and management review.

Precise3D's manufacturing facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified by an accredited registrar. This certification covers the full product lifecycle: design and development, supplier management, incoming inspection, production, final testing, packaging, and post-sale support.

Annual surveillance audits and a full recertification every three years ensure the quality management system remains effective. Internal audits are conducted quarterly across all departments, with findings tracked to closure in a CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Action) system.

For distributors, ISO 9001 provides confidence that our quality claims are backed by a systematic, audited framework — not ad-hoc inspection at the end of the line. Your customers can verify our certification status through the registrar's public database.

ISO 9001:2015 certified facility: documented processes from design to post-sale support

Documented Processes

Every production procedure, inspection criterion, and test protocol is documented, version-controlled, and accessible at the workstation. No tribal knowledge — every operator follows the same validated procedure, every time.

Internal Audit Program

Quarterly internal audits across all departments. Trained internal auditors follow ISO 19011 guidelines. Audit findings logged in CAPA system with root cause analysis and corrective action tracked to verification of effectiveness.

Management Review

Monthly management review of quality KPIs: DOA rate, warranty claim rate, supplier performance, audit findings, CAPA aging. Strategic decisions on resource allocation and process improvement driven by data, not intuition.

Country-Specific Requirements

Beyond the Major Certifications

Major certifications cover 80% of markets. For the remaining 20%, we manage country-specific requirements so distributors do not have to.

Energy Efficiency

PSU complies with DOE Level VI (USA), ErP Lot 6 (EU), and MEPS (Australia). Standby power consumption under 0.5W. External PSU efficiency over 88% at full load. Test reports from accredited labs available.

Radio Equipment

WiFi module pre-certified: FCC ID (USA), ISED (Canada), and RED Directive 2014/53/EU (Europe). Modular approval allows integration without additional radio testing. Bluetooth coexistence tested for adjacent-channel immunity.

Country-Specific Marks

Additional marks available on request for specific markets: EAC (Eurasian Economic Union), BIS (India), SABS (South Africa), SASO (Saudi Arabia), INMETRO (Brazil). Lead time 8 to 12 weeks for new country-specific certifications.
