Assembly Process

Six Stages to a Finished Printer

Every Precise3D printer passes through six controlled stages. Standardized work instructions, operator sign-off at each gate, and serial-number traceability throughout.

1. Frame Fabrication & Inspection

CNC-machined aluminum extrusions cut to length and deburred. Laser-cut steel panels with precision-drilled mounting points. Every frame measured against 0.1mm tolerance across all mounting surfaces. Out-of-spec components rejected at source — zero rework accepted.

2. Electronics Sub-Assembly

Controller boards assembled on our SMT line. All connectors crimped and labeled. Wiring harnesses fabricated to length with strain relief. Each board undergoes powered burn-in testing for 2 hours minimum. Stepper driver current calibrated per motor specification.

3. Motion System Assembly

Linear rails mounted and aligned on granite surface plate. Gantry assembled and squared to within 0.05 degrees. Belt routing with tension verified to 50 Hz +/-5 Hz on acoustic tension meter. All bearings lubricated and checked for smooth travel with zero binding.

4. Final Assembly & Integration

Electronics bay integrated into frame. Heated bed mounted and leveled. Extruder/hotend assembly installed. Enclosure panels fitted with acoustic dampening. Wiring routed through cable chains. Full system integration verified — all subsystems communicating on first power-up.

5. Calibration & Test Print

Auto bed leveling mesh verified with max deviation under 0.1mm. PID tuning for hotend and bed. Extruder e-steps calibrated. Standardized test print: 20mm calibration cube, 50mm bridging, 0-60 degree overhang, retraction tower. Professional series runs 4-hour continuous print. All results logged per serial number with timestamp.

6. Packaging & Pre-Shipment

Custom high-density EPE foam inserts CNC-cut per model. Double-wall export-grade cartons with reinforced corners. Desiccant packs and humidity indicator cards inserted. Accessories checklist verified: spool holder, toolkit, USB drive with software, user manual, test print sample included. Palletized with edge protectors and stretch wrap.

Quality Integration

QC Embedded in Production

Quality is not a separate department — it is embedded at every stage. In-process inspection stations are positioned directly on the assembly line.

IQC Station

Incoming materials inspected at receiving dock. Sampling per AQL 1.0 normal. Full batch inspection for critical components: linear rails, bearings, belts. Non-conforming materials quarantined and returned to supplier within 24 hours.

IPQC Stations x 6

In-process quality checkpoints after each assembly stage. Go/no-go fixtures for quick pass/fail decisions. Detailed inspection for first article of each production batch. IPQC data feeds into daily SPC dashboard.

OQC Station

Pre-shipment audit per AQL 0.65 Level II. Packaging integrity verified. Certificate of conformance issued. Serial numbers registered in warranty database. Test print log included in every box.
