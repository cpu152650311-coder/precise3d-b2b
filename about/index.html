About Us

Precision Engineering.
Global Reach.

Founded in 2011 in Shenzhen, China — the heart of global electronics manufacturing — Precise3D has grown from a small R&D team into a trusted OEM partner for distributors in over 50 countries.

Our Story

Precise3D started with a simple belief: that high-quality 3D printing should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. What began as a five-person engineering team in a Shenzhen workshop has grown into a 15,000m² ISO 9001-certified manufacturing facility producing over 30,000 units per month.

Our team of 200+ engineers, technicians, and quality specialists work at the intersection of precision mechanical engineering and user-centered design. Every printer that leaves our factory undergoes 48 hours of continuous testing before packaging.

We don't just build printers — we build partnerships. Our dedicated account managers work directly with distributors to customize product configurations, packaging, and support materials for each target market.

Quality That Scales

Every Precise3D printer passes through a rigorous 7-stage quality control process before leaving our factory. From incoming component inspection to 48-hour burn-in testing, we ensure consistency at any production volume.

Our in-house testing lab includes temperature chambers, vibration tables, and automated print-quality inspection systems. We track defect rates in real-time and maintain a 99.7% on-time delivery rate with less than 0.5% DOA across all shipments.
